Who We Are

We are The Intern Project. Our mission is to foster a community of interns by creating and sharing events and resources that benefit the internship experience. Originating in the summer of 2013, we've been back every summer since to host picnics, curate dine-outs, and work behind the scenes to help y'all take advantage of your summer internships!
